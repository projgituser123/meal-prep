Very personalized. Plans are generated from the profile data you provide (age, height, weight, activity level), your stated goals (lose/fat/gain/maintain/athletic performance), and explicit preferences (cuisine, meal frequency, time to cook). You can further tune portion sizes, swap individual meals, and set target macro ratios manually if you prefer. Plans update automatically when you change goals or activity level.

The system will avoid excluded items when generating recipes and flag potential allergens on each recipe card. Note: for severe allergies or medical dietary requirements (anaphylaxis, celiac disease), always verify ingredient sourcing and consult a healthcare professional.

Estimates are calculated using standard formulas (BMR + activity multiplier) and ingredient nutrition databases, and they are suitable for everyday planning and fitness progress tracking. Accuracy depends on input quality (body metrics, activity level) and recipe fidelity—expect typical variance of ±5–10%. This tool is intended for guidance; if you have specialized performance goals or medical conditions, consult a registered dietitian or physician before making major changes.
